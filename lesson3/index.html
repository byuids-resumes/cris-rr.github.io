Site Plan

Site name

Cloudy Zone

Site Purpose

"Cloudy Zone" purpose is to deliver accurate information about the weather on any location, in real-time, allowing people to share their local conditions and provide tips through the site. it should be a reference in this field.

Cloudy Zone will accomplish this offering the weather for the current location and the possibility to add favorites places to the personal profile, later the site will let group people like families or friends, facilitating the weather conditions of the closest ones.
